About Us

Khoos Trading Pty Ltd, trading as Alphapet, is an established pet care company operating in Australia, specialising in pet boarding, pet grooming, and pet-related retail services. We currently operate three centres across Victoria.

Our headquarters in Mornington, together with our St Kilda centre, are primarily dedicated to professional cat boarding services. These centres provide high-quality cattery facilities, offering safe, comfortable, and professionally managed accommodation for cats, along with daily care and attentive welfare support while their owners are away.

Our Montrose centre focuses primarily on pet grooming services, catering to a broad range of customer needs in pet care services.

Over the years, Alphapet has built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality pet care services across all locations. Our continued success is founded on our commitment to animal welfare, professional service standards, and customer satisfaction.

Pet Grooming Services

From extensive bath services to quick maintenance trims, Alphapet is here to lend you a paw with all your cat grooming and dog grooming needs.

Our team is passionate about caring for all your four-legged friends. With years of experience working in the industry, our members have had the privilege of working with all kinds of pets across Australia and overseas. Whether it's an energetic dog or a cute timid cat, the Alphapet team will be more than happy to give your furry friend the grooming, care and attention that they deserve.

Your pet's welfare is our number ONE priority, and this is what separates us from others. Throughout the grooming session, we'll make sure your pet is comfortable and safe. With Alphapet, you can rest easy knowing that your furry friend is in good hands.
